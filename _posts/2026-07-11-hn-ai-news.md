          - Reve 2.1 scores 1306 Elo on Arena.ai, up 36 points from Reve 2.0 in one
            month, landing second overall and top-3 in six subcategories including product
            branding, cartoon, and multilingual text rendering.
          - The layout-first approach converts every text prompt into a structured scene

          - Reve 2.1 outperforms Nano Banana Pro and FLUX.2 on Arena's foreign-text
            rendering category, a historically weak spot for diffusion models.
        discussion_bullets:

          - Skepticism emerged around the Arena.ai Elo system, with users noting that
            pairwise human-vote leaderboards favor aesthetic appeal over reliability for
            production use cases.

          - Context window is 262K tokens by default, extendable to 1,010,000 tokens via YaRN (a context-extension
            technique); natively multimodal with an integrated vision encoder.
          - Thinking Preservation retains and reuses prior reasoning traces across multi-turn agent sessions, reducing
            redundant token generation and improving consistency.
        discussion_bullets:
          - Users confirmed the Q4_K_M quantization (~16.8 GB) fits on an RTX 4080/4090 or M4 Pro 24 GB+ at 25-50 tok/s;
            llama.cpp or LM Studio recommended over Ollama, which lacks vision support.
          - "Community excitement: SWE-bench 77.2% puts it within ~3.7 points of Claude Opus 4.6 (80.8%) at zero API cost
            with full local control."
          - Qwen3.6-27B beats Gemma 4 31B on coding with less VRAM (~18 GB vs 24 GB+ at 4-bit); a Qwen3.6-35B-A3B
            mixture-of-experts sibling offers lower active-parameter inference for those wanting it.

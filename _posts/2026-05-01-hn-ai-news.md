      a Google-specific AI model into the web platform an abuse of market position.

      so the API targets a neutral baseline rather than a proprietary one — though no such coordination mechanism currently
      exists.'

      widely, capturing how suppressing one behavior through fine-tuning can surface another undesirable one elsewhere.
